Youtube Spam Comments Detection

Using Bernoulli Naive Bayes algorithm

Group 8

Helping Content Creators with Spam Filtering Technology

Spam detection is essential for ensuring the quality and credibility of online platforms like YouTube. Spam comments not only disrupt genuine conversations but also pose risks such as spreading misinformation and deceiving users. By implementing effective spam detection systems, platforms can maintain a conducive environment for meaningful interactions and protect users from potential harm. Furthermore, spam detection is crucial for content creators, as it helps them preserve the integrity of their content and maintain audience trust. Overall, spam detection plays a pivotal role in upholding the integrity, security, and user experience of online communities and content-sharing platforms.

Algorithm

The Bernoulli Naive Bayes algorithm, an extension of the Naive Bayes classifier, simplifies text classification tasks by assuming independence among binary features. Its historical roots trace back to Thomas Bayes' work in the 18th century, evolving into a key component of modern machine learning. Its simplicity facilitates broad accessibility and interpretation, making it a popular choice for spam detection, sentiment analysis, and document classification. Despite its simplicity, it offers effective and efficient performance, handling real-time applications and large datasets with ease. Its wide applicability across diverse domains underscores its enduring importance in the field of text mining and machine learning.
